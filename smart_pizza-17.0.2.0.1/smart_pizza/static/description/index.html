Smart Pizza Module

Your solution for efficient pizza order management in Odoo

About Smart Pizza

Smart Pizza is an Odoo module that streamlines pizza order processing, enhances menu management, and improves customer service for pizzerias.

Features

  • Order Management: Easily track and manage orders from start to finish.
  • Courier Shift Management: Easily track and manage courier's shifts from start to finish.
  • Menu Customization: Create and update your pizza menu effortlessly.
  • Customer Database: Maintain a detailed record of customer orders and preferences.
  • Real-time Inventory: Automatically update inventory as orders are processed.
  • Sales Reports: Generate reports to analyze sales and improve business decisions.
